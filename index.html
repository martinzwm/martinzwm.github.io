About Me

I am a master's student in the department of Chemical Engineering at Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). I am passionate about developing computational models for autonomous laboratory. Building on top of the current research in robotic chemist, I wish to develop a robot capable of independently navigating the enormous chemical search space, designing and synthesizing a potentially newly discovered molecule, collecting experimental results, and iterating until the desired material properties are satisfied.

Before coming to MIT, I graduated from the Univeristy of Waterloo with major in Chemical Engineering, two minors in Artificial Intelligence and Management Science, and a specialization in Process Modelling, Optimization and Control. I wish to become an interdisplinary engineer, I have pursued diverse research and industrial experiences during undergraduate in the areas of autonomous vehicle, wireless sensing, energy sector, and more.
